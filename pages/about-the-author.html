About The Author

Hi, my name is Andrew Kail and I am a full-stack engineer and Python developer in Aiken, South Carolina. I'm originally from Tennessee where I went to school at the University of Tennessee Knoxville where I received my Masters in Aerospace Engineering.

I have started this blog as a way for me to explore the software stacks behind our favorite websites and applications. I love to tinker with various applications and this is just an extension of that.

Most of the posts here should be tutorial like in nature and I will try to keep them update to date as best as I possibly can. The blog software itself has been written by me in Python and I plan on testing some of the tools and workflows on this app.

Thank you for stopping by and reading my blog and I hope you learn something new while you're here.

Andrew
